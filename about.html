A lot of people haven’t worked with an architect in the past and we help guide them through that process. Architects as a profession are the managers of a building project. There's a team of qualified people required to succeed on all levels.

One of the things I like best about being an architect is every project is different. It's this wonderful combination of both art and science. What I do is custom buildings for each unique site that we want to last for a long time and work perfectly.

I love the challenge that every new project brings. It’s a chance to try new ideas, to look at options, to maybe apply some of the new technologies and materials and approaches that are out there.

When you enjoy what you do then good things can happen.

Our speciality is local. Since 2004 our office in the Comox Valley has provided ready access to building sites on Vancouver Island and along the BC coastline.
