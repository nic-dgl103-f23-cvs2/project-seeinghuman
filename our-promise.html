Our promise.

Architecture is successful when it is functional, practical, beautiful, durable and uniquely suited to its place.

Mastery.

With 20 years of experience, we specialize in creating architecture that is aesthetically pleasing and meets the unique needs of our clients in the BC Coastal environment.

We are committed to achieving excellence in architectural consulting services.

 

Capability.

Our team is familiar with local bylaws and climate, ensuring that your project is well-suited to its environment. We are ALWAYS looking for better ways to do things.

 

Professional.

We enthusiastically embrace the challenge of delivering design strategies and project management that responds pragmatically to our client’s requirements. High standards in environmental stewardship are a guiding principle of our practice.

As a LEED® Accredited Professional Phillipa strives to craft energy-efficient and environmentally sensitive buildings.

 

Reputation.

We have a proven track record with commercial, residential and small institutional projects for new construction and renovations. No project is too small to benefit from good design.

We understand the process of translating the needs of our clients into beautiful and successfully functioning environments.
